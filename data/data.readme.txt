Data used in the paper:
- Sample period: 2000Q1–2024Q4
- Out-of-sample evaluation: 2015Q1–2024Q4 (40 forecast origins)
- Variables: Real GDP growth, consumption growth, investment growth, CPI inflation, federal funds rate, credit spread, NFCI, VIX + 3 additional macro-financial series
- All series seasonally adjusted and sourced from FRED, BEA, BLS, Chicago Fed
- cleaned_data/ contains the exact dataset used in estimation and forecasting
